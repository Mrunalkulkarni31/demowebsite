Students Performance Analytics

Students are the main assets for various schools, colleges and universities. Students play an important role in the academic as well as none academic overall performance of the institution, ...... The level of achievement of students and indirectly of school can be measured and tested through examination, assessments and other forms of measurements. However, academic performance achievement varies as different kinds of students may have different levels of performance. The student academic performance is usually stored in the student management system in different formats such as files, documents, records, images and other formats. This useful information could be used to predict the students’ overall performance!

Teachers Performance Analytics

A teacher is one of the most important entities while considering school analytics which has a direct impact on the student performance. Thus, better the teacher’s performance, better is the school and student performance and analytics of this performance will help gain useful insights for improvisation from the data., ...... The performance standards have been identified such as Designing learning experiences for children, knowledge and understanding of subject matter, strategies for facilitating learning, Interpersonal relationship, professional development, school development and teacher attendance. If the teacher performs task in an innovative way and makes extra efforts for improving student performance, she/he can be rated as beyond the expected standard. Self- assessment by the teacher should be done at least twice in year, one ending first quarter and second ending third quarter.

Teaching-Learning Performance Analytics


Teaching Analytics is Whhat refers to the methods and digital tools to help teachers analyze and improve the educational designs prior to the delivery. Furthermore, more recent developments on Teaching analytics also support analysis of how the teacher delivers the educational designs ...... and Learning Analytics Which refer to the methods and digital tools that allow the measurement, collection, analysis and reporting of data about learners and their context for purposes of understanding and optimizing learning and the environments in which it occurs. The potential of teaching analytics to analyze the educational designs in the constituent elements (learning and assessments activities and educational resources/tools) and the interrelations between the elements. The potential learning analytics to measure, collect, analyze and report on learner’s educational data and the learning context that they are generated aiming to improve the learning conditions for individual learners or group of learners.
